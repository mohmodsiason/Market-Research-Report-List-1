<p><p>The ventilator rental market comprises several types, primarily categorized into axial flow, centrifugal, and others. Axial flow ventilators operate by moving air parallel to the axis of the fan, providing efficient air circulation with lower energy consumption. Centrifugal ventilators, on the other hand, have blades that move air radially, offering higher pressure and are suitable for industrial applications. The "others" category encompasses various specialized devices used in specific clinical settings, enhancing patient care and ventilation strategies in diverse medical environments.</p></p>
