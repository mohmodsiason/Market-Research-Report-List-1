<p><p>The Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) market is divided into two main categories: independent (dedicated) GPUs and integrated GPUs. Independent GPUs are standalone units that offer high performance for gaming, 3D rendering, and complex computations, appealing to gamers and professionals. In contrast, integrated GPUs are built into the computer's motherboard or CPU, providing essential graphics capabilities for general tasks while being cost-effective and energy-efficient. This division caters to different user needs, balancing performance with budgetary and power considerations.</p></p>
