<p><p>The dietary chocolate products market comprises three primary types: white chocolate, dark chocolate, and milk chocolate. White chocolate, made from cocoa butter, sugar, and milk solids, lacks cocoa solids, resulting in a creamy, sweet flavor. Dark chocolate, rich in cocoa solids and lower in sugar, is often sought for its health benefits due to its higher antioxidant content. Milk chocolate contains a blend of cocoa solids, milk, and sugar, offering a milder taste and creamier texture, appealing to a broad audience.</p></p>
