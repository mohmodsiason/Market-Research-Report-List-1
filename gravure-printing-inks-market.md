<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Gravure Printing Inks</li><li>Water-based Gravure Printing Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gravure printing inks market comprises two primary types: solvent-based and water-based inks. Solvent-based gravure inks utilize organic solvents for their formulation, offering rapid drying times and vibrant color quality, making them ideal for flexible packaging. In contrast, water-based gravure inks use water as a primary solvent, promoting eco-friendliness and compliance with stringent environmental regulations. They are generally suited for substrates like paper and cardboard, providing good adhesion and lower volatile organic compound emissions, thus appealing to environmentally conscious consumers.</p></p>
