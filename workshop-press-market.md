<p><p>Workshop Press markets are categorized into four main types: manual, electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Manual presses rely on human effort for operation, offering simplicity and portability for small tasks. Electric presses enhance efficiency and consistency, suitable for production environments requiring precise control. Pneumatic presses utilize compressed air, providing high speed and force, ideal for assembly lines. Hydraulic presses leverage fluid pressure, delivering immense strength for heavy-duty applications, often used in industrial settings. Each type serves distinct operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
