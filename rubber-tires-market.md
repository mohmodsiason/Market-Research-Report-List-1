<p><strong>The Rubber Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tires</li><li>Radial Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber tires market is primarily categorized into two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature plies that overlap each other at angles, providing a flexible structure, making them ideal for certain off-road applications and lower-speed vehicles. In contrast, radial tires have plies arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, offering better handling, fuel efficiency, and durability, making them the preferred choice for modern vehicles. Each type caters to specific performance and usage requirements in the automotive sector.</p></p>
