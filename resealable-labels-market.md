<p><p>Resealable labels are used across various packaging types to enhance convenience and freshness. Flow wrap packs are flexible, allowing easy sealing and resealing for snacks or baked goods. Tray seal packaging is commonly used for fresh produce and ready-to-eat meals, providing airtight seals. Gas-flush thermoforming involves replacing air with gases to prolong shelf life. High Pressure Processing (HPP) packs ensure food safety while retaining freshness, and vacuum packs remove air to preserve items longer, preventing spoilage.</p></p>
