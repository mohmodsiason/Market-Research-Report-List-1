<p><p>The Electronically Commutated (EC) fans market comprises various types, including centrifugal, axial, mixed flow, and cross flow fans. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to move air, suitable for high static pressure applications. Axial fans move air parallel to the axis of rotation, ideal for low-pressure systems. Mixed flow fans combine characteristics of both types, offering versatility. Cross flow fans deliver air across a flow path, commonly used in compact spaces. Each type benefits from energy efficiency and precise speed control inherent in EC technology.</p></p>
