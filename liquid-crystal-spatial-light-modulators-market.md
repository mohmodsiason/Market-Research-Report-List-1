<p><ul><li>Reflective Spatial Light Modulators</li><li>Transmissive Spatial Light Modulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Crystal Spatial Light Modulators (LC SLMs) are classified into two main types: reflective and transmissive. Reflective SLMs utilize a light-reflecting surface to modulate incoming light, making them ideal for applications requiring high brightness and contrast, such as projectors and optical devices. In contrast, transmissive SLMs manipulate light as it passes through them, offering advantages in applications involving precise image processing, such as holography and microscopy. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including entertainment, telecommunications, and biomedical imaging.</p></p>
