<p><p>Presenters in the market can be categorized into Infrared and Radio Frequency types. Infrared presenters utilize infrared signals to communicate with the connected device, requiring a direct line of sight and typically used in smaller ranges. In contrast, Radio Frequency presenters operate over longer distances, using radio waves, which allows for movement without a direct line of sight. Both types are popular tools for presenters, enhancing audience interaction and control during presentations while catering to different user preferences and settings.</p></p>
