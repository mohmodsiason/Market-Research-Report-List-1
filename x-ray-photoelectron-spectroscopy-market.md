<p><p>X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy (XPS) market can be categorized into non-destructive and destructive types based on the impact on the sample. Non-destructive XPS preserves the integrity of the specimen, allowing for repeated analysis without altering its surface characteristics. This type is ideal for exploring delicate materials or coatings. Conversely, the destructive XPS involves sample modification or consumption during analysis, often yielding high-resolution data. This approach is typically utilized for bulk materials or when surface alterations don't affect subsequent applications.</p></p>
