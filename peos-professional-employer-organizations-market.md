<p><p>Professional Employer Organizations (PEOs) provide comprehensive HR services by co-employing a client's workforce, allowing companies to outsource payroll, benefits, compliance, and risk management. The Full-Service PEO model manages nearly all HR functions while sharing employer responsibilities. In contrast, the Administrative Services Organization (ASO) market offers more limited services, primarily focusing on administrative tasks like payroll processing and benefits management without the co-employment aspect. Businesses choose between these models based on their specific HR needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
