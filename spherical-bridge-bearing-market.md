<p><ul><li>Fixed Type</li><li>Guided Type</li><li>Free Sliding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spherical Bridge Bearing market comprises three primary types: Fixed Type, Guided Type, and Free Sliding Type. Fixed Type bearings restrict movement in all directions, ensuring stability in structures. Guided Type bearings allow controlled movement in specific directions, accommodating rotational and lateral loads. Free Sliding Type bearings facilitate unrestricted horizontal and vertical movement, ideal for structures experiencing expansion and contraction. Each type serves distinct structural needs, influencing design choices in bridge construction and engineering projects.</p></p>
