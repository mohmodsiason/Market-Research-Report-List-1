<p><p>The single crystal growing furnace market encompasses various types of technologies used for crystal growth. The Czochralski Process (Cz) Furnace is widely used for producing large, high-quality single crystals, especially silicon. The Float Zone (FZ) Furnace offers a high-purity method by growing crystals in a floating zone, minimizing contamination. The Bridgman Furnace is utilized for growing crystals at controlled rates, ideal for certain materials. Other methods include the Vapor-Liquid-Solid (VLS) and Hydrothermal processes, catering to specific applications and materials.</p></p>
