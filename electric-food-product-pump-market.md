<p><strong>The Electric Food Product Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Peristaltic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric food product pump market features primarily two types: centrifugal and peristaltic pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize a rotating impeller to move liquids, making them efficient for high-flow applications and suitable for thin, non-viscous food products. In contrast, peristaltic pumps operate by compressing a hose to push fluid forward, making them ideal for handling viscous or shear-sensitive products. Both types are essential in food processing and packaging, catering to diverse applications and ensuring efficient product transfer while maintaining hygiene.</p></p>
