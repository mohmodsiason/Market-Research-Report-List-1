<p><p>The vascular graft market includes several key types based on material composition. ePTFE (expanded polytetrafluoroethylene) vascular grafts are known for their biocompatibility and low thrombogenicity, making them suitable for long-term use. Polyester vascular grafts offer strength and flexibility, often utilized in peripheral applications. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) vascular grafts are valued for their durability and resistance to kinking. Other graft materials may include synthetic and biological options, each catering to specific clinical needs and patient conditions in vascular surgery.</p></p>
