<p><p>The biometric sensor market comprises several types, each utilizing different technologies. Capacitive sensors detect fingerprints by measuring changes in capacitance. Ultrasound sensors use sound waves to capture detailed skin and fingerprint information. Optical sensors capture images of fingerprints using light, while thermal sensors detect patterns of heat emitted by skin. Electric field sensors measure changes in electric fields, often used for touchless biometric applications. Each type caters to specific applications in security, authentication, and user interaction across various industries.</p></p>
