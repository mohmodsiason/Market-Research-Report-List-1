<p><p>The NB-IoT Gas Meter market is primarily categorized into two types: NB-IoT Ultrasonic Smart Gas Meters and NB-IoT Smart Diaphragm Gas Meters. Ultrasonic smart gas meters utilize ultrasonic technology for precise gas flow measurement, offering high accuracy and reduced maintenance costs. In contrast, smart diaphragm gas meters employ a diaphragm mechanism to measure gas flow, ensuring reliability and ease of installation. Both types leverage Narrowband Internet of Things (NB-IoT) connectivity for enhanced data transmission and smart monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
