In contrast, the lug type has threaded lugs on the valve body, allowing for easy installation and removal without affecting the pipeline. Both types enhance operational flexibility and reliability, with the wafer type ideal for tight spaces and the lug type suitable for systems requiring maintenance access.</p></p>
