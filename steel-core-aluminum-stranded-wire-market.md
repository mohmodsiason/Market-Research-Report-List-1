<p><p>The Steel Core Aluminum Stranded Wire (ACSR) market includes several types: ACSR, ACSR/AW, and ACSR/TW. ACSR consists of aluminum strands wrapped around a central steel core, providing high strength and conductivity, suitable for overhead power transmission. ACSR/AW features an additional aluminum wire layer, enhancing the wire's strength and corrosion resistance. ACSR/TW offers a thicker, twisted structure that increases durability and improves resistance to environmental factors. Each type is designed for specific applications in electrical and telecommunications infrastructures.</p></p>
