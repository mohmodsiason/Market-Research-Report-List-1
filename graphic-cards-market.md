<p><ul><li>Discrete Graphics Card</li><li>Integrated Graphics Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The graphics card market is primarily divided into two types: discrete graphics cards and integrated graphics cards. Discrete graphics cards are standalone units designed for high-performance gaming, graphic design, and intensive computational tasks, offering superior performance and dedicated memory. In contrast, integrated graphics cards are built into the CPU, sharing system memory, making them suitable for everyday tasks like browsing and basic multimedia. The choice between the two depends on user needs, from casual use to demanding graphical applications.</p></p>
