<p><ul><li>Wafer Cassette</li><li>Wafer Frame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer carrier market comprises two primary types: wafer cassettes and wafer frames. Wafer cassettes are enclosed containers designed to protect and store silicon wafers during handling and transportation, offering a secure environment to minimize contamination. In contrast, wafer frames provide a rigid structure for holding and supporting wafers during processing, ensuring stability and precision. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, facilitating the safe and efficient movement of wafers through various stages of production.</p></p>
