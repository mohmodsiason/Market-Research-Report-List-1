<p><strong>The Forged Steel Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forged Steel Check Valves</li><li>Forged Steel Globe Valves</li><li>Forged Steel Gate Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forged steel valves market consists of various types, including forged steel check valves, globe valves, and gate valves. Forged steel check valves prevent backflow by allowing fluid to flow in one direction, ensuring system integrity. Globe valves regulate flow with a spherical body design, providing precise control. Gate valves, designed for fully open or closed positions, offer minimal fluid restriction and are ideal for on/off applications. Together, these valves are essential in industrial applications for maintaining flow control and safety.</p></p>
