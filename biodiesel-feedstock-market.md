<p><p>Biodiesel feedstocks are primarily categorized into three market types: vegetable oil-based biodiesel, animal fat-based biodiesel, and used cooking oil (UCO). Animal fat-based biodiesel derives from livestock by-products, offering a sustainable alternative that leverages waste materials. UCO is collected from restaurants and food processing, recycling waste into valuable fuel. Each type contributes to a diverse, eco-friendly biodiesel market, promoting sustainability.</p></p>
