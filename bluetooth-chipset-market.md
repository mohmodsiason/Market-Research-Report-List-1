<p><strong>The Bluetooth Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode</li><li>Dual Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth chipset market is categorized into two main types: Single Mode and Dual Mode. Single Mode chipsets support only Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), making them ideal for applications requiring low power consumption and reduced data transfer needs, such as wearables and IoT devices. In contrast, Dual Mode chipsets can handle both Classic Bluetooth and BLE, offering versatility for devices needing robust data transfer alongside low energy operations, such as smartphones and various consumer electronics.</p></p>
