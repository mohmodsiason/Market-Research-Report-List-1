<p><p>The Semiconductor Parametric Test System market is categorized into static and dynamic parameter testing. Static parameter testing evaluates fixed electrical characteristics of semiconductor devices, such as voltage, current, and resistance, under stable conditions. In contrast, dynamic parameter testing assesses a device's performance under varying conditions, measuring parameters like frequency response and switching speed. Both testing methods are crucial for ensuring semiconductor reliability and functionality, catering to different phases of device development and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
