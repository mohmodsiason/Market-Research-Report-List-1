<p><ul><li>Course Computer (Processor)</li><li>Drive Mechanism</li><li>Control Head</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine autopilot systems comprise various components that enhance navigation and control on vessels. The course computer (processor) acts as the system's brain, processing navigational data and executing steering commands. The drive mechanism physically steers the vessel, powered through hydraulic or electric systems. The control head provides user interface options for operators to set course parameters and monitor performance. Other market segments may include sensors, user interfaces, and additional integration tools, contributing to the overall efficiency of marine navigation systems.</p></p>
