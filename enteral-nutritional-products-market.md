<p><ul><li>Enteral Nutritional Powder</li><li>Enteral Nutritional Emuslison</li><li>Enteral Nutritional Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enteral nutritional products market comprises three main types: enteral nutritional powders, emulsions, and suspensions. Enteral nutritional powders are dehydrated formulas that can be mixed with liquids for patients unable to consume solid food. Emulsions are liquid products that provide a balanced mix of nutrients, including fats, proteins, and carbohydrates, often designed for easy absorption. Enteral nutritional suspensions are thicker liquid formulations that offer a similar nutrient profile as emulsions, ideal for those with specific dietary needs or preferences.</p></p>
