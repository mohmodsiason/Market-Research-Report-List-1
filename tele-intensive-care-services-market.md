Centralized tele-ICUs involve a hub-and-spoke model, where critical care specialists monitor multiple remote ICUs from a central location, enhancing efficiency and expertise. Decentralized models allow local ICU teams to utilize telemedicine tools for immediate consultation and support, promoting timely decision-making. Other models may incorporate hybrid systems or specialized services tailored to specific needs, fostering flexibility and better resource allocation in critical care environments.</p></p>
