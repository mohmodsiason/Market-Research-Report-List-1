<p><strong>The PVC Insulated Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVC Sheathed Armoured Cable</li><li>PVC Sheathed Unarmoured Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC insulated cable market comprises two main types: PVC sheathed armoured cables and PVC sheathed unarmoured cables. PVC sheathed armoured cables are designed for enhanced protection, featuring an additional layer of armor that safeguards against mechanical damage and environmental factors, making them suitable for rugged installations. In contrast, PVC sheathed unarmoured cables are lighter and more flexible, ideal for indoor applications or where there is limited physical risk. Both types utilize durable PVC insulation for effective electrical performance and safety.</p></p>
