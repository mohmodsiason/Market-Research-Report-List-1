<p><ul><li>Solid Insulation</li><li>Gas Insulation</li><li>Air Insulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ring Main Unit (RMU) market is categorized into three primary types based on insulation methods: Solid Insulation, Gas Insulation, and Air Insulation. Solid Insulation RMUs use materials like epoxy resin to provide reliable performance and durability. Gas Insulation RMUs utilize SF6 (sulfur hexafluoride) gas, offering compact designs and enhanced safety in high-voltage applications. Air Insulation RMUs rely on air as an insulating medium, making them cost-effective and easier to maintain, but typically suitable for lower voltage applications. Each type varies in scalability, maintenance needs, and application suitability.</p></p>
