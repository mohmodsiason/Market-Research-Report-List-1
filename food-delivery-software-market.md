<p><p>Food delivery software can be categorized into three main types: web-based, on-premise, and managed. Web-based solutions are accessed online, offering flexibility and ease of use without installation. On-premise software is installed locally on a business’s hardware, providing more control and customization but requiring maintenance. Managed services combine both, where the software is hosted by a provider, handling updates and support while still allowing businesses to customize their interface. Each type serves different operational needs and preferences within the food delivery sector.</p></p>
