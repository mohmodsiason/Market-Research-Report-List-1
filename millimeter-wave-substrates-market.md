<p><p>The millimeter-wave substrates market is categorized into several types, each serving distinct applications. LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) substrates offer excellent thermal stability and low loss, making them suitable for high-frequency circuits. PI (Polyimide) substrates provide robust thermal and chemical resistance, ideal for flexible and high-performance electronics. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) substrates exhibit low dielectric loss and high chemical resistance, suitable for high-frequency applications. Other substrates may include ceramics or specialty polymers, catering to various demands in the millimeter-wave technology landscape.</p></p>
