<p><p>Heart-lung bypass equipment consists of technologies used during cardiac surgeries to maintain blood circulation and oxygenation when the heart is temporarily stopped. The market is segmented into single roller pump and double roller pump equipment. Single roller pump cardiopulmonary bypass equipment employs one roller pump to manage blood flow, offering simplicity and efficiency. In contrast, double roller pump equipment utilizes two pumps to enhance flow stability and reduce trauma to blood cells, providing a more reliable option for complex procedures.</p></p>
