<p><p>The Electronic Funds Transfer Point of Sale (EFTPOS) terminals market encompasses three primary types: Counter-Top Terminals, Mobile Terminals, and Inbuilt Terminals. Counter-Top Terminals are stationary devices typically found at retail checkout counters, allowing customers to complete transactions securely. Mobile Terminals are portable devices enabling businesses to process payments anywhere, enhancing convenience for on-the-go service. Inbuilt Terminals are integrated into existing systems, such as tablets or kiosks, offering seamless transaction capabilities and saving space in retail environments. Each type caters to specific operational needs and customer preferences.</p></p>
