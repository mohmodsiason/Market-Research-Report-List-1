<p><strong>The Sn-Cu-PET Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sn-Cu-PET foil market can be divided into two primary types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by physically rolling copper into thin sheets, offering uniform thickness and enhanced mechanical properties, making it suitable for various applications. In contrast, electrolytic copper foil is created through an electrochemical process, resulting in finer grain structures and better conductivity, which is ideal for high-performance electronics. Each type serves distinct needs within the electronics industry, emphasizing their unique properties.</p></p>
