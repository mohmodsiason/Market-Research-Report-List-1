<p><strong>The Wavemeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Michelson Interferometer-Based</li><li>Fizeau Interferometer-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wavemeter market features two primary types: Michelson interferometer-based and Fizeau interferometer-based systems. Michelson interferometers utilize the principle of beam splitting and path differences to measure wavelengths with high precision, making them suitable for applications in optics and metrology. In contrast, Fizeau interferometers use a flat reference surface and are typically favored for measuring optical components' surface quality and flatness. Both types serve critical roles in precision measurement across scientific research, telecommunications, and industrial applications.</p></p>
