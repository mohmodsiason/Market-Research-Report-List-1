<p><p>The electric bus charging infrastructure market comprises various systems, primarily depot charging, city charging, and column charging. Depot charging involves installing charging stations at bus depots, where vehicles are charged overnight or during breaks, ensuring high capacity and efficiency. City charging, on the other hand, integrates charging facilities into urban environments, allowing buses to recharge during their routes at designated points. Column charging involves standalone charging points, often installed along streets, facilitating quick replenishment of buses at any time.</p></p>
