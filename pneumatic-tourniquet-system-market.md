<p><p>The Pneumatic Tourniquet System market features two main types: Single Bladder Tourniquet and Dual Bladder Tourniquet. Single Bladder Tourniquets utilize a single inflatable chamber to exert pressure on a limb, effectively controlling blood flow during surgical procedures. In contrast, Dual Bladder Tourniquets have two inflatable chambers, allowing for more precise control of pressure and enhancing safety by reducing the risk of tissue damage. Both types play crucial roles in various surgical settings, improving patient outcomes through effective blood management.</p></p>
