<p><ul><li>Constant Energy Magnets</li><li>Pulsed Electro-Magnetic Field Energy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic therapy devices market consists of two main types: constant energy magnets and pulsed electro-magnetic field (PEMF) energy devices. Constant energy magnets provide a steady magnetic field, which is believed to promote healing and alleviate pain through continuous magnetic exposure. In contrast, PEMF devices emit varying frequencies and intensities of magnetic fields, designed to stimulate cellular repair and enhance overall wellness. Both types are used for therapeutic purposes, targeting conditions like chronic pain, inflammation, and stress management.</p></p>
