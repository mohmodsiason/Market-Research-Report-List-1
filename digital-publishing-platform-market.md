<p><p>Digital publishing platforms can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions operate over the internet, allowing users to access and manage content from anywhere, offering scalability and reduced IT overhead. In contrast, on-premises platforms are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control and customization, but requiring more maintenance and infrastructure investment. Both options cater to different business needs, with cloud solutions typically favored for flexibility, while on-premises options suit organizations prioritizing data security.</p></p>
