<p><p>Pilot control devices serve as essential components in various automation and control systems, facilitating precise operation and monitoring. The market for these devices is categorized into primary control devices and pilot control devices. Primary control devices include essential equipment like sensors and transmitters that directly manage processes. In contrast, pilot control devices act as intermediary units, managing the signals to larger actuators or systems, ensuring seamless operation and enhancing system reliability and efficiency in industrial applications.</p></p>
