<p><p>The optical spectrometers market is categorized into two primary types: atomic spectrometry and molecular spectrometry. Atomic spectrometry focuses on measuring the light emitted or absorbed by atoms, making it ideal for detecting elemental compositions in various samples. Conversely, molecular spectrometry analyzes the interactions of light with molecules, providing insights into chemical structures and concentrations. These techniques are essential in fields such as environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and material science, driving advancements in analytical capabilities and precision.</p></p>
