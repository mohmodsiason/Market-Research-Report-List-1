<p><ul><li>Glucose Oxidase</li><li>Glucose Dehydrogenase</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diabetes test strips market is primarily categorized into three types: Glucose Oxidase, Glucose Dehydrogenase, and Other. Glucose Oxidase strips utilize an enzyme that oxidizes glucose, producing an electrical signal proportional to glucose levels, making them widely used for their accuracy. Glucose Dehydrogenase strips employ a different enzyme that can operate in the presence of other sugars, providing versatile testing options. The "Other" category includes alternative technology and less common materials, catering to niche markets and personalized monitoring solutions.</p></p>
