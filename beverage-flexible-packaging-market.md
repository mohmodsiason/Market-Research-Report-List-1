<p><p>The Beverage Flexible Packaging market comprises various materials, each serving distinct purposes. Polyethylene (PE) and Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) are popular for their moisture resistance and flexibility, ideal for drink pouches. Polypropylene (PP) offers excellent barrier properties and is often used for bottles and caps. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) is lightweight and recyclable, commonly used for water and soft drink bottles. Polyvinylidene Chloride (PVDC) provides superior moisture and gas barriers, enhancing product shelf life, particularly for sensitive beverages.</p></p>
