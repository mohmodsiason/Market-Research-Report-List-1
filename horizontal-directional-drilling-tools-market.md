<p><p>The Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) tools market encompasses various types, including drill bits, reamers and openers, drill pipes and rods, among others. Drill bits are designed for initial penetration and cutting through various soil types. Reamers and openers expand the borehole to accommodate pipelines. Drill pipes and rods provide the necessary connection and support between the surface and the drill bit. Other tools may include locators and fluids, enhancing the drilling process and ensuring precise navigation and stability.</p></p>
