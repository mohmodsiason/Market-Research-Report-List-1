<p><ul><li>Fixed Shaft Type</li><li>Straight Shaft Type</li><li>Externally Driven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid stepper motor linear actuators are categorized into three types: Fixed Shaft Type, Straight Shaft Type, and Externally Driven Type. The Fixed Shaft Type features a stationary housing with an integrated lead screw, providing compact and precise motion for applications where space is limited. The Straight Shaft Type offers a direct linear motion approach with an extended shaft, suitable for longer travel distances. The Externally Driven Type relies on external components to drive the actuator, allowing flexibility in design while maintaining efficient operation.</p></p>
