<p><p>The laminated PP woven bag market consists of three main types: open pocket, roll pocket, and valve pocket bags. Open pocket bags feature a wide opening for easy filling and are ideal for bulk products. Roll pocket bags allow for efficient stacking and can be rolled down for smaller openings, enhancing convenience during use. Valve pocket bags are designed with a one-way valve, enabling quick filling while preventing spillage, making them suitable for fine powders and granulated materials.</p></p>
