DAS enhances coverage and capacity by using multiple antennas connected to a central source, ideal for large venues. V-RAN utilizes virtualization to optimize radio resources and reduce costs, enabling flexible deployment and management of network infrastructure, thus enhancing user experience in complex environments.</p></p>
