<p><ul><li>Mechanical Differential Pressure Gauge</li><li>Digital Differential Pressure Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cleanroom Differential Pressure Gauge market is divided into two main types: mechanical and digital differential pressure gauges. Mechanical differential pressure gauges utilize traditional analog technology, providing reliable measurements through Bourdon tubes or diaphragm systems. They are often preferred for their simplicity and durability. Digital differential pressure gauges, on the other hand, employ advanced electronic sensors for accurate, real-time readings, offering features like data logging and remote monitoring. Both types are essential for ensuring optimal cleanroom environments by monitoring pressure differentials effectively.</p></p>
